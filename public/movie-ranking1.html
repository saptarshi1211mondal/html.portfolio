The Best Web Series According to Saptarshi

My top 3 web series of all-time.

1. Stranger Things

This is my favourite si-fi web series. I love the story

2. Money Hiest

Really cool web series. Eight thieves take hostages and lock themselves in the Royal Mint of Spain as a criminal mastermind manipulates the police to carry out his plan.

3. Jurassic World Camp Cretaceous

This is my favourite anime series. Six teens invited to attend a state-of-the-art adventure camp on Isla Nublar must band together to survive when the dinosaurs break out of captivity.
